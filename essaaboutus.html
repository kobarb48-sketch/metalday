Misión

Metal Day S.A. tiene como objetivo principal la recuperación y la valorización máxima de todos los residuos que gestiona. Clasificamos y acondicionamos los materiales para su posterior reciclaje, dando una segunda vida a los residuos que generamos.

Visión

Metal Day S.A. busca consolidarse como una empresa líder en su sector, ofreciendo un trato profesional, cordial, personalizado y eficiente a sus proveedores y clientes. Crecemos mediante contacto fluido con instituciones similares en Europa y Asia.

FAQ

Preguntas Frecuentes

Resolvemos tus dudas sobre nuestros servicios y procesos de reciclaje

What does METAL DAY, S.A. do?

METAL DAY, S.A. is a leading company in the recyclable waste market with over 25 years of experience. We are dedicated to buying and selling ferrous and non-ferrous metals (scrap) nationally and internationally, importing and exporting worldwide, general consulting, and clearing apartments, premises, warehouses, and factories.

Where are they located?

Our facilities are located on Carrer Cobalto in L'Hospitalet de Llobregat (Barcelona). We are only 15 minutes from the Port of Barcelona, which allows us to offer exceptional logistics advantages for imports and exports.

How can I contact them?

Monday to Saturday from 8:30am to 8:30pm, you can also visit us directly at our facilities.

Do you sell wholesale or only retail?

We work both wholesale and retail. For wholesale purchases, we export to destinations such as Pakistan, UAE, India, Turkey, and Egypt with a supply capacity of over 2,000 tons annually. For individual sellers, we offer competitive prices and immediate payment.

Do you have a scale to weigh the materials?

Yes, we have an 18-meter scale for trucks and vans up to 60 tons, and two small scales of 2 tons for individual suppliers and companies with smaller quantities. Weighing is done accurately and transparently.

How do I get paid if I sell scrap metal?

We offer multiple payment options for your convenience: cash payment for smaller amounts, bank transfer for larger amounts, and we also accept payment by bank check. Payment is made immediately once the transaction is completed and the material is verified. For companies, we also offer invoicing options with agreed payment terms.

How long does it take them to pay?

For cash sales, payment is immediate: cash at the time of delivery, or bank transfer that is processed the same day or the next business day. For companies with invoicing, payment terms are agreed upon in advance according to established commercial conditions.

Do you provide loading and unloading facilities?

Yes, we provide loading and unloading facilities for all customers visiting our site to buy or sell materials. Our team is available to assist in handling your goods safely and efficiently.
